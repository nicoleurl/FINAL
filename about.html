PURPOSE

LOCALS is a project that seeks to highlight the small businesses of the Colonial Zone of Santo Domingo in the Dominican Republic that offer quality services and products. In order to support companies and provide visitors with a unique experience of Dominican customs.

We guarantee the best possible experience of the Colonial Zone of the Dominican Republic for all its visitors. We do this by sharing with you all the internal details about local issues that you would not normally know but that are special to the people in the area. By using our knowledge of this part of the city, we created a route and made a list of the recommended places to choose according to what you are looking for.

Our goal is to share with you, to help you have a more genuinely Dominican experience, making you feel as if you were just another local. You can not stop trying. In this way we help you enjoy and help small businesses grow. Great combination, right? Apart from this we have implemented the great idea of ​​adding a party train in which you can visit all these places while you are on board. As if this weren't enough you have the option of walking and touring the area on your own and at your pace. Not only that, with our help you can always consult the selected places with our app. For more information keep browsing our page. It's impossible not to love it.

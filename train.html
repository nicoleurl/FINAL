Our Train

Our main attraction is our train. It circles around Zona Colonial going by all the stops we have selected. It starts its journey at 9 a.m. every day and takes about 10 mins. to get from stop to stop and the whole route takes about 2 h.

Whats the fun part? This is not a normal train, it’s a party train. We help you start your night in the Zona early with a free Mamajuana shot, a typical dominican drink, when you get on and you’ll be able to enjoy some dominican music inside while you get to your next stop. Party like a true dominican, with a bar and enough space to dance, while you go around the area.

You don’t even have to get down anywhere you can stay on all night to party with us, it’s like you’ll have your own personal moving club whenever and wherever you need. we’re like your own personal moving club. This will also help you void the hassle of having to walk home after a long night of partying, we will make sure we leave you at whatever hotel from our route you picked.
